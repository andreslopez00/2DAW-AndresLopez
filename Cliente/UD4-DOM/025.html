Horse

The horse is one of two extant subspecies of Equus ferus. It is an odd-toed ungulate mammal belonging to the family Equidae.

Donkey

The donkey or ass is a domesticated member of the horse family, Equidae. The wild ancestor of the donkey is the African wild ass.

Cat

The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae.
